Style target:
- Naturalistic, concrete, and specific.
- One coherent paragraph or a short multi-paragraph description.
- Usually compact, but long enough to make the stakes, constraints, and relationships legible.
- Prefer concrete institutional, interpersonal, temporal, or logistical details over abstract exposition.
- The conflict should be embedded in the facts of the situation, not explained from above.

Generation priorities, in order:
